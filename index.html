Fundamentals

Essential principles every miniature painter should know.

01
Thin your paints — two thin coats always beats one thick coat. Aim for milk consistency.
02
Prime first — black = dark & muted. White/grey = bright & vibrant. Match to desired outcome.
03
Base → Shade → Layer → Highlight — the core workflow for painting any miniature.
04
Equivalents are approximate — undertone, opacity and finish vary between brands.
05
Washes go into recesses — they provide instant depth and definition to any model.
06
Edge highlight — a fine line of lighter colour along edges brings a model to life.
